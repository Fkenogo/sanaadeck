## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Brand Identity
**Platform Name:** SanaaDeck  
*(Sanaa = Art/Craft in Swahili + Deck = Team/Department)*

### Core Positioning
**"East Africa's Structured Creative Department"**

SanaaDeck is NOT:
- ❌ A freelance marketplace
- ❌ A traditional agency
- ❌ An unlimited design service

SanaaDeck IS:
- ✅ A subscription-based creative production system powered by credits
- ✅ A structured, dashboard-driven workflow platform
- ✅ A curated team operating as your outsourced creative department

### Value Proposition
**For Clients:** Predictable monthly costs, transparent credit system, quality creative work, local understanding, flexible local payments

**For Creatives:** Consistent income stream based on production time, clear performance metrics, professional platform, credit-based earnings

**For the Business:** Scalable credit-based model, manageable costs through freelancers, protected margins, measurable operations

## CORE BUSINESS MODEL - CREDIT ECONOMY

### What Is a Credit?

**1 credit = 45 minutes of focused production time**

#### Credits Represent:
- ✅ Production effort and time
- ✅ Complexity and skill required
- ✅ Graphic design work
- ✅ Illustration work
- ✅ Motion graphics
- ✅ Copywriting (if requested)

#### Credits DO NOT Represent:
- ❌ Deliverable size
- ❌ File count
- ❌ Visual simplicity
- ❌ Number of revisions

**Key Principle:** Credits measure **production effort**, not output quantity.

### Freelance Creative Management

#### Onboarding Process
1. **Application Review:** Portfolio + skills assessment
2. **Test Project:** Paid trial (1-2 credits worth)
3. **Interview:** Video call for culture fit + availability
4. **Onboarding Training:** Platform use, quality standards, communication
5. **Tier Assignment:** Junior, Mid, or Senior (determines payout rate)
6. **Full Access:** Can receive project assignments

#### Payment Structure

**Credit-Based Payouts:**
- Junior: $7 per credit
- Mid-level: $9 per credit
- Senior: $11 per credit

**Payment Terms:**
- Weekly batch payments (every Friday)
- Direct to M-Pesa or bank account
- No delays, no holds

**Admin & Account Managers:**
- Fixed monthly pay
- Not paid per credit
- Salary-based compensation

**Incentive System:**
- **Bonus:** Extra 10% for 5-star client ratings
- **Fast-track Bonus:** +20% for 24-hour completions (rush projects)
- **Volume Bonus:** Complete 20+ credits/month = +5% on all credits
- **Consistency Bonus:** Maintain 4.5+ rating for 3 months = +10% rate increase

#### Creative Accountability

**Capacity Management:**
- Maximum: 120 credits per month per creative (realistic cap)
- Ideal: 90-100 credits to avoid burnout
- Monitoring: Weekly utilization tracking

**Performance Metrics (CPS - Creative Performance Score):**
- Client rating ≥ 4.2/5
- On-time delivery ≥ 95%
- Revision ratio ≤ 35%
- Missed deadlines ≤ 2/month
- 3 validated complaints → formal review

**Escalation Path:**
- Warning → Probation → Removal
- No emotional overrides
- Data-driven decisions

## RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION

### Business Risks

**Risk 1: Credit System Confusion**
- **Mitigation:** Clear credit guide, examples, video tutorials
- **Contingency:** Offer onboarding calls for first month

**Risk 2: Credit Exhaustion Frustration**
- **Mitigation:** Proactive alerts at 80% usage, easy extra pack purchase
- **Contingency:** Flexible upgrade mid-month without penalty

**Risk 3: Scope Creep (Margin Erosion)**
- **Mitigation:** Strict credit confirmation before work, no free changes
- **Contingency:** Admin oversight, automated alerts for unusual patterns

## APPENDIX: KEY TERMINOLOGY

**Credit:** 45 minutes of focused creative production time

**Dashboard-Only Engagement:** Policy requiring all client-creative interaction through platform (no WhatsApp/email for design work)

**Creative Pod:** Team structure of 1 senior + 2 mid designers + shared specialists handling 8-12 clients

**CPS (Creative Performance Score):** Automated metric tracking designer quality, speed, revision rate

**Extra Credit Pack:** Add-on purchase of 10 credits for $250 when monthly allocation exhausted

**Credit Utilization Rate:** Percentage of allocated credits actually used by clients (target: 70-85%)

**Gross Margin:** Revenue minus direct creative costs (target: 50%+)

**Margin Leakage:** Untracked work or scope creep that erodes profitability

**Template Library:** Internal collection of reusable design assets, components, and frameworks
